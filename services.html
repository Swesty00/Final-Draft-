Services

Westhart Deer Meats offers full deer processing, including skinning, cutting, grinding, and packaging, along with specialty items like sausage, jerky, and snack sticks. For those who want to process their own deer, the company provides DIY kits with all the necessary tools and clear instructions. They also create step-by-step educational videos and offer practical tips on game handling and meat preservation, making them a complete resource for both professional processing and at-home learning.
