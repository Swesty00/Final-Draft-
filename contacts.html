Buisness Background

Shane Westhart and Trey Stutheit, founders of Westhart Deer Meats, grew up hunting together and learning to process their own game from an early age. Their shared passion for the outdoors and helping others led them to start a business focused on affordable deer processing, DIY kits, and simple educational resources that teach hunters how to process their own meat. They take pride in offering quality work and making the tradition of processing game more accessible for everyone.
